Frequently Asked Questions
What is a class action settlement?
A class action settlement is compensation paid by a company to people affected by a lawsuit. If a company violated consumer laws or wronged customers, a judge may approve a settlement pool for affected people to claim their share.
How much money can I get?
Payouts vary by settlement. Amounts range from $10 to several thousand dollars depending on the case, the settlement fund size, and how many eligible claims are filed. Check each settlement for specific payout information.
Is there a deadline to claim?
Yes, each settlement has a claim deadline. Claims must be submitted by the deadline shown on the settlement page. Missing the deadline means losing your right to compensation, so act quickly!
Is ClaimCheck free?
Yes, 100% free. ClaimCheck is a free resource funded by ads. We never charge to find or claim settlements. Filing claims with settlement administrators is also always free.
Why should I trust ClaimCheck?
We compile settlement information from court filings and official settlement administrator websites. We display deadlines, eligibility requirements, and payouts transparently so you can make informed decisions. We're not attorneys and don't provide legal advice.
