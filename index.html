Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if I qualify for a class action settlement?
Each settlement has specific eligibility requirements. Visit our settlement pages to check if you purchased the product, used the service, or were affected during the qualifying time period.
Is filing a claim really free?
Yes. Filing a class action claim is always free. You never pay anything out of pocket. Settlement administrators handle the process and distribute payments to eligible claimants.
How much money can I get from a class action settlement?
Payouts vary widely. Some settlements pay $5 to $50 per claimant, while major ones like the Capital One breach paid up to $25,000. The amount depends on the total fund and number of claimants.
How long does it take to receive a settlement payment?
Most settlements take 3 to 12 months after the claim deadline to distribute payments. Complex cases may take longer. ClaimCheck tracks deadlines so you never miss a filing window.
Can I file claims on behalf of family members?
In many cases, yes. Spouses and immediate family members may be eligible if they purchased the product or were affected. Each settlement has different rules, so check the specific case requirements.
What if I miss the filing deadline?
Most settlements have hard deadlines. However, some allow claims after the deadline in special circumstances. ClaimCheck tracks all deadlines so you can claim before they close.
