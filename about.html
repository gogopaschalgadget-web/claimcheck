About ClaimCheck

Our Mission

ClaimCheck helps people find and claim money from class action lawsuit settlements. Class action settlements recover billions of dollars annually, but many people are unaware of them or don't know how to file claims. We make the process simple and transparent.

Our Source

We aggregate settlement information from legitimate public sources including:

  • TopClassActions.com - Settlement directory
  • ClassAction.org - Official settlement listings
  • JAMS and AAA arbitration portals
  • Federal court settlement databases
  • Settlement administrator websites

Our Values

Legitimacy

We only list settlements from verified sources. Every settlement is cross-referenced with official court records and settlement administrator websites.

Transparency

We clearly disclose all eligibility requirements, estimated payouts, and deadlines. We never hide fees or surprise requirements.

User Privacy

We respect your privacy. We never sell or share personal information. Our site uses encrypted connections to protect your data.

No Conflicts of Interest

We are not affiliated with settlement administrators, attorneys, or companies involved in settlements. Our only goal is to help you find and claim money you're owed.

Legal Information

ClaimCheck is not a law firm and does not provide legal advice. Settlement information is provided for educational purposes only. Users are responsible for verifying all settlement information independently.

We are not affiliated with any court, settlement administrator, or legal entity. Each settlement is independently managed by court-appointed administrators.

Contact

For questions about specific settlements, contact the settlement administrator directly. Links to official settlement websites are provided on each settlement page.
